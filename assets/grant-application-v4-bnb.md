## What is Backchain

Backchain is a **modular, unstoppable ecosystem designed to bridge Web2 and Web3** — serving as a hub that brings everyday people into the world of cryptocurrencies and decentralized services, without requiring them to understand blockchain technology first.

At its core, Backchain is **Real-World Application (RWA) infrastructure**: a set of interconnected smart contract modules that solve real problems — certifying documents, expressing yourself freely, predicting outcomes, raising funds transparently, and earning yield — all running natively on opBNB with sub-cent transaction costs.

**What makes Backchain fundamentally different:**

- **100% community-owned.** There is zero token reserve for developers, founders, or investors. Not 5%, not 1% — literally zero. Every BKC token in existence is earned through real ecosystem usage. The founder earns the same way any user does — by participating.

- **Self-financing by design.** Backchain introduces a new model where the ecosystem funds its own growth without angel investors, VCs, or presales. Protocol fees flow to operators (developers who build frontends), tutors (people who refer new users), buyback (deflationary pressure), and treasury (sustainability). Every participant is financially incentivized to grow the ecosystem — creating a **self-sustaining viral loop** where growth funds more growth.

- **Unstoppable.** Every module runs entirely on-chain. No server can be shut down, no company can block access, no government can censor content. Once deployed, Backchain works forever — even if the original team disappears.

- **Modular.** Backchain is not a single app — it's a collection of independent modules that can be combined in any configuration. An operator can build a pure social platform (Agora only), a document certification service (Notary only), or a full DeFi suite with everything. Each combination targets a different audience, a different market, a different use case.

**The vision:** Millions of people worldwide need services that blockchain delivers better than any centralized alternative — censorship-resistant communication, tamper-proof document proof, transparent fundraising, fair prediction games. Most of these people have never touched crypto. Backchain is the bridge that brings them in — not through financial speculation, but through **real utility they can't get anywhere else.**

### 2. FortunePool — Fair Predictions for Everyone (Prediction Markets)

**The problem it solves:** Prediction and betting platforms are centralized, opaque, and exclusionary. They set the odds, control the outcome verification, take 10-30% margins, and are banned in most countries. People want to predict and wager — it's human nature — but they can't trust the house.

**What FortunePool is:** A 3-tier commit-reveal prediction game running entirely on-chain with variable odds:

| Tier | Entry | Odds | Audience |
|------|-------|------|----------|
| Bronze | 0.001 BNB (~$0.60) | 1/5 → 2x payout | Casual — play for fun, highest win rate |
| Silver | 0.01 BNB (~$6) | 1/10 → 5x payout | Regular — balanced risk/reward |
| Gold | 0.1 BNB (~$60) | 1/150 → 100x payout | Serious — lottery-style massive payouts |

**Why it's a world-changing differentiator:**

- **Provably fair.** Commit-reveal scheme using on-chain block hashes. No house advantage in outcome determination. The smart contract IS the house — and the smart contract can't cheat.
- **Front-running impossible.** Players commit their prediction before the determining block is mined. Even miners can't manipulate the outcome because the commit is cryptographically sealed.
- **Instant resolution.** No waiting for off-chain oracles or manual settlement. The blockchain resolves every prediction automatically.
- **Accessible worldwide.** No KYC, no country restrictions, no geo-blocking. Anyone with a BNB wallet can play — from a student in Nigeria to a trader in Singapore.
- **Variable odds per tier.** Bronze (20% win rate) keeps casual players engaged. Gold (100x payout) creates viral "I won $60 for $0.60" moments perfect for social sharing.
- **Micro-stakes enabled by opBNB.** The Bronze tier at 0.001 BNB (~$0.60) is only possible because opBNB transaction costs are negligible. On Ethereum L1, the gas fee would exceed the bet itself.

**How it reaches millions:**

```
Crypto influencer tweets "I just won 0.1 BNB on FortunePool — provably fair, no KYC"
→ Followers try the Bronze tier for fun ($0.60 — less than a coffee)
→ Social sharing ("I won!" / "I lost but it was fair") creates viral loops
→ Each player becomes a Tutor referral — their friends join too
→ Gaming communities, Telegram groups, TikTok creators all discover it
```

Prediction games are inherently viral — people love sharing wins and challenging friends. FortunePool's Bronze tier removes the financial barrier ($0.60 to try), and the Tutor system rewards anyone who brings players. **Every streamer, every gaming community, every Telegram group becomes a growth channel.**

**For BNB Chain:** Prediction games generate high-frequency transactions — multiple plays per session, multiple sessions per week. 10,000 active FortunePool players could generate **50,000+ weekly transactions** on opBNB, each paying BNB fees that flow to operators, tutors, and buyback.

### 3. Notary (Cartorio Digital) — Proof That Lasts Forever (RWA)

**The problem it solves:** Proving that a document existed at a specific moment is critical for contracts, intellectual property, legal disputes, academic credentials, and business records. Traditional notary services are expensive ($50-200 per document), slow (requires physical presence or mail), limited by jurisdiction, and dependent on centralized institutions that can be compromised, corrupted, or destroyed.

**What Notary is:** An on-chain document certification system. The user generates a cryptographic hash of any file locally (the file never leaves their device), and the hash is recorded on opBNB with a timestamp and metadata. The result: **permanent, tamper-proof, globally verifiable proof that a specific document existed at a specific moment** — for a fraction of a cent.

**Why it's a world-changing differentiator:**

- **Works anywhere, 24/7.** No office hours, no jurisdiction limits, no bureaucracy. A farmer in rural Brazil can certify a land agreement at 2 AM on a Sunday.
- **Privacy total.** Only the hash goes on-chain — not the document. It's mathematically impossible to reconstruct the document from the hash. The document stays private with the owner.
- **Verification is free, forever.** Anyone can check if a document was certified by generating its hash and querying the contract. No account, no fee, no registration — forever.
- **Batch certification.** Up to 20 documents in a single transaction. Perfect for law firms, accounting firms, compliance departments, and any organization handling document volume.
- **Asset certification.** Beyond documents — certify photos, videos, audio, and any digital asset with a guided wizard. Art authentication, evidence preservation, intellectual property registration.
- **Costs less than a penny.** On opBNB, certifying a document costs a fraction of a cent. Compare: a traditional notary charges $50-200. This is not 10% cheaper — it's **99.99% cheaper**.
- **Transferable.** Certifications are ERC-721 tokens that can be transferred between wallets — enabling ownership changes, corporate reorganizations, and document handoffs.
- **Instant verification.** Public verification page — anyone can check a certification by hash or ID without creating an account, forever.

**How it reaches millions:**

```
Law firm discovers they can certify contracts for <$0.01 instead of $50
→ They become an Operator, building a legal-tech certification platform
→ Their clients (businesses, individuals) create BNB Chain wallets to use it
→ Each client becomes a Tutor referral — recommends to business partners
→ Accountants, real estate agents, schools, hospitals discover the service
→ Each profession builds its own certification vertical
```

Unlike DeFi modules that appeal to crypto-native users, **Notary appeals to the entire professional world** — lawyers, accountants, doctors, teachers, real estate agents, corporate compliance officers. These are people who may never have heard of BNB Chain but desperately need affordable, reliable document certification.

**The developing world opportunity:** In countries where traditional notary services are expensive, corrupt, or inaccessible (much of Latin America, Africa, Southeast Asia), on-chain certification is not just cheaper — it's the **only reliable option**. A small business owner in Nigeria who can't afford $200 to notarize a partnership agreement can now certify it on-chain for less than a penny. **This is Web3 solving a real problem that affects billions of people.**

**For BNB Chain:** Document certification is a **recurring, high-volume activity**. Businesses certify documents weekly or daily. A single law firm processing 100 certifications/month generates steady transaction volume. Scale this to thousands of professional users worldwide, and Notary alone could drive **hundreds of thousands of monthly opBNB transactions**.

## The Self-Financing Model: Zero Token Reserves, Pure Community Ownership

Traditional crypto projects reserve 15-30% of tokens for the team, 10-20% for investors, and 5-10% for advisors. Backchain reserves **zero** — for anyone.

**How BKC tokens come into existence:**

Every BKC token is minted through "Proof-of-Purchase Mining" — users earn BKC proportional to the BNB fees they pay when using the ecosystem. No pre-mine, no presale, no private rounds. The only way to get BKC is to **use** the ecosystem or **buy** it from someone who did.

**200M hard cap** with scarcity curve — as supply grows, mining yields decrease. At 160M supply, users earn only 25% of fees paid in BKC. This creates natural scarcity and deflationary pressure.

**How the ecosystem funds itself without investors:**

```
User pays BNB fee on any action (stake, certify, predict, post, trade...)
├── 10-20% → Operator (developer who built the frontend — their salary)
├── 10% → Tutor (person who referred the user — their commission)
├── 30-50% → Buyback & Burn (buys BKC from DEX, burns it — deflationary)
└── 10-30% → Treasury (protocol sustainability — no individual controls this)
```

**No one needs to invest.** Operators earn by building apps. Tutors earn by referring users. Users earn BKC by participating. The protocol sustains itself through usage fees. **This is a completely new model — the ecosystem is simultaneously the product, the business, and the distribution channel.**
